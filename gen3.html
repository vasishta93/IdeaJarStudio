Building Visibility and Leads for gen@3 with a Minimum Viable Growth Plan

Kerala's First Overseas Education and Immigration Consultancy

  • Client: gen@3
  • Year: 2025
  • Industry: Overseas Education Consultancy

Overview

gen@3, Kerala’s first overseas education and immigration consultancy, has over 18 years of experience helping individuals fulfill their dreams of studying or settling abroad. Despite strong offline success, they faced challenges in establishing a robust online presence to increase brand awareness and generate leads.

What They Wanted

The founders sought to translate their trusted reputation into an impactful online presence. Their primary goals were to:

  • Build brand awareness.
  • Generate qualified leads.
  • Develop a marketing and content strategy to maximize limited resources.

Roadblocks That Hindered Their Growth

  • Lack of cohesive branding: The brand lacked a clear visual and verbal identity to resonate with online audiences.
  • Unclear messaging: Website and social media communication failed to connect with target audiences effectively.
  • Inefficient website design: Poor navigation, unclear user journeys, and weak CTAs contributed to high bounce rates.
  • Unfocused marketing strategy: Efforts lacked direction, with limited success in generating visibility or leads.
  • Resource constraints: A small team and tight budget required a streamlined yet effective strategy.

Full-Funnel Marketing Strategy Approach to Drive Growth


Laying the Foundation for a Strong Brand Presence

To position gen@3 for long-term success, we began by emphasizing their legacy of trust and expertise, ensuring alignment with their audience’s aspirations and pain points.

Branding and Strategy Creation

  • Visual and Verbal Identity: We crafted cohesive branding reflecting gen@3’s trustworthiness and 18+ years of expertise.
  • Content Communication Guidelines: Developed a consistent tone and voice aligned with the audience’s aspirations of studying or settling abroad.

Audience Persona Mapping

  • Created detailed buyer personas for both students and immigrants, addressing their specific challenges and dreams.
  • Identified key platforms and touchpoints where these personas actively seek information.

Competitor and Platform Analysis

  • Thorough competitor analysis to uncover gaps and position gen@3 distinctively.
  • Evaluated platform relevance, focusing on Facebook, Instagram, and webinars.

Optimizing the Website Experience

  • Website Audit: Identified UI/UX and navigation issues that hampered engagement and conversions.
  • Messaging and Copy Revamp: Reworked homepage messaging to speak directly to visitor aspirations, building trust and encouraging action.
  • SEO Framework and Keyword Strategy: Centered on pillar and cluster keywords to boost organic traffic and align with buyer intent.
  • Guidance for UI/UX Improvements: Recommended mobile responsiveness, streamlined navigation, and engaging visuals.

Creating a Phased Growth Plan


Organic Content Strategy

  • Developed an impactful mix of educational resources, client success stories, and posts addressing audience pain points.
  • Boosted high-value posts to extend reach and engagement.

Lead Generation via Ads and Webinars

  • Launched targeted Meta ad campaigns tailored to specific buyer personas.
  • Executed strategic WhatsApp marketing campaigns for direct, personalized outreach.
  • Hosted webinars addressing common questions about studying or immigrating abroad, fostering trust and engagement.

Future Plans

  • Creating SEO-driven blogs to bolster organic traffic and thought leadership.
  • Leveraging LinkedIn to connect with professionals and enhance credibility.
  • Scaling webinar efforts to engage larger audiences and drive more conversions.

Measuring Success and Refining the Strategy

  • Profile Views & Impressions: Tracking visibility across social channels.
  • Clicks & Calls Booked: Gauging audience engagement and interest.
  • Webinar Signups: Evaluating lead generation and conversion potential.


Impact in 3 Months

  • 44k views on a single post, significantly boosting visibility.
  • 55.6% increase in overall visibility across platforms.
  • Two high-ticket conversions achieved.
  • 17 leads added to the pipeline, showcasing growing interest.

Wrapping Up

With a clear brand identity, optimized website, and targeted marketing strategies, gen@3 is now equipped to thrive in the digital space. Their revamped online presence has enhanced brand awareness and successfully generated qualified leads. As we continue our collaboration, we’ll focus on accelerating their growth and solidifying their authority in the education consultancy sector across more audience touchpoints.
